API end points: 
For entire categories: https://www.nichea.co.za/nichea/products/1/10/ASC/name/CATEGORY_NAME?keyword=
    It's route: products/CATEGORY_NAME

For a single product: https://www.nichea.co.za/products/name/{name}
    It's route: products/PRODUCT_NAME

For all products: https://www.nichea.co.za/nichea/products/1/10/ASC/name?keyword=
    It's route: products/all
