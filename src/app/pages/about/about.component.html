Nichéa started in 1991 as an industrial cleaning chemical supplier.

Nichéa chemicals has supplied resellers and contract cleaners with quality cleaning chemicals. Since we started we have constantly adapted and improved our cleaning chemicals to meet market demands. This means that there are regularly new cleaning products added to our range.

Our range of  chemicals include: Floor CareHygiene and disinfectantLaundry careCleaning equipmentLuxurious body careHousehold careFRAGRANCE

Nichéa offers its own label cleaning chemicals as well as doing contract supplying for a wide variety of clients. We are able to supply both liquid and powder cleaning chemicals.

Nichea offers SABS approved cleaning chemicals and equipments from the comfort of your home, office or holiday. Search, Select, Add to Basket and Buy Online.

Fast Delivery Nationwide.

For orders over R250, Nichea delivers free of charge within South Africa, either door-to-door, door-to-counter, depending on where you’re located. For orders under R250, Nichea charges a non-refundable R50 fee to deliver either door-to-door or door-to-counter, depending on where you’re located. If you reside outside of a main centre but would like door-to-door delivery you can select that delivery option for a non-refundable R200 delivery fee.
